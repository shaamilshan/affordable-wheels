Who We Are ?

We are Affordable Wheels, your reliable partner for sourcing good quality cars.
Founded in 2020 by a group of auto enthusiasts who are passionate about cars, we have successfully delivered several vehicles and built a strong base of satisfied customers across Kerala.
Affordable Wheels was born from a shared love for automobiles. Abin Mathew, Govind Santhosh, and Vishnu Sajeevan envisioned a platform that helps people find their dream cars without breaking the bank. Operating since 2020, we have an established network in Delhi to ensure quality sourcing, and we also offer complete documentation services including NOC and KL registration upon request.
